# How to Manage Users from the Collate Support Portal

Collate provides an intuitive Support Portal where admins can manage organization members, assign roles, and control access levels.

## Applies To

All Collate customers.

## Managing Users

1. Log in to the Support Portal at [https://portal.usepylon.com/get-collate/login](https://portal.usepylon.com/get-collate/login)  
2. You must have an **Admin** role to manage members  
3. To add a new member:  
   - Click on **Members**  
   - Click **Add Members** to invite new users

## Explanation of Roles

### **Admins**

- Can view and manage all tickets across the organization  
- Can change teammates’ roles  
- Can invite additional users to the portal  

### **Members**

- Can only view and manage their own tickets  
- Cannot view coworkers’ tickets  
- Cannot change roles or invite teammates  

### **No Access**

- Users with no portal access (cannot log in)  
- Admins can revoke or restore access at any time
